Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-15

Contact: support@YOURDOMAIN.com

Summary

VolleyScore is designed to work without collecting personal data. The app stores scoreboard settings and state locally on your device.

Data we collect

We do not collect or sell personal data.

  • No account required
  • No analytics SDK required
  • No advertising tracking

Data stored on your device

The app may store the following locally on your device:

  • Scoreboard state (scores, games won)
  • Settings (e.g., haptics on/off, starting score preference)
  • Trial start date (stored locally, may use Keychain for reliability)

In-App Purchases

If you choose to purchase the lifetime unlock, the transaction is handled by Apple’s App Store. We do not receive your payment details. Apple may provide us with purchase entitlement information so the app can unlock features.

Third-party services

VolleyScore does not integrate third-party analytics or advertising services by default. Apple’s services may be involved in processing in-app purchases and restoring purchases.

Children

VolleyScore is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your choices

  • You can delete the app to remove locally stored data.
  • For purchase issues, you can restore purchases via the app and/or manage subscriptions/purchases through your Apple ID.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page with a new effective date.
