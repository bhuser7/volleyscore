FAQ

How do I change the score?

  • Swipe up on a team side to add 1.
  • Swipe down to subtract 1.

How do I reset?

  • Tap Menu → Reset → choose Reset game / Reset sets / Reset all.

How does the trial work?

  • VolleyScore includes a 30-day free trial.
  • After the trial ends, you can unlock lifetime access for a one-time fee.

I paid—how do I restore?

  • On the paywall, tap Restore Purchase.
  • Make sure you’re signed into the same Apple ID that purchased the unlock.

Haptics not working

  • Menu → Settings → toggle Haptics.
  • Also confirm your device is not in Silent mode (some devices/settings affect feedback).
